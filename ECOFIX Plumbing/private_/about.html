Who We Are

Founded in 2019, EcoFix Plumbing Services is a Gauteng-based company committed to sustainable plumbing solutions. We specialize in water-saving technologies and eco-conscious installations for both residential and commercial clients.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable plumbing services while reducing environmental impact. We believe in combining technical excellence with green innovation to build a better future.

Why Choose Us?

  • Eco-friendly materials and methods
  • Transparent pricing and honest service
  • Community-focused and locally owned
